This is a Lord of the Rings inspired memory match. Enemies will spawn in the middle of the screen when the player matches incorrectly. Overtime, more powerful enemies will begin to spawn. Your goal is to correctly match all cards before your Hit points reach 0;
Shows remaining hit points.
Shows remaining armor. On enemy attack, armor will be deducted before hit points.
Upon matching the Summon Rohan card, the player will gain access to a one time use ability, Horn of Rohan. This ability allows the player to clear all enemies currently on the board, preventing them from attacking. After matching the cards correctly, the Horn of Rohan will begin to pulse at the bottom of the screen. To use the ability, simply click on the horn. Use this ability wisely!
Upon matching the Dwarven Healer card, the player will gain 8 hit points
Enemy attack values are displayed on their respective cards. The value highlighted in red is amount of damage the player will take from that particular card.
Matching the Nazgul card will summon a Nazgul to the battlefield. This Nazgul is the strongest enemy in the game with 4 attack points. Try and wait till the end of the game to correctly match this card!
